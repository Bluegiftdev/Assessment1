4. Sasa Host

In the Kenyan web hosting landscape, Sasahost Limited emerges as a seasoned player and among the best web hosting companies in Kenya. It brings over a decade of experience since its establishment in 2009.

Diverse Hosting Services

Sasahost caters to a wide clientele with its array of services, including shared web hosting, domain registration, VPS, dedicated servers, and security solutions like SSL certificates and Immunify360.

Pricing Structure

Sasahost offers various packages, starting from Cpanel Basic web hosting plans priced between Ksh 3,000 and Ksh 11,000 per year. For those seeking dedicated servers, the cost ranges from Ksh 22,000 to Ksh 47,000 per month, providing options that align with diverse hosting needs.
